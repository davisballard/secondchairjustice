## Compensation Structure

### Profit Split
- **33% Davis Ballard**
- **33% Alex DuBelko**
- **33% Sasha Zinshtein**

Applied to all net profit after direct costs (ad spend, tool subscriptions, platform fees).

### How It Works
1. Revenue comes in from attorney partners purchasing leads at $400/lead
2. Subtract direct costs (ad spend, platform fees, tool subscriptions)
3. Net profit splits 33/33/33

## How the Three Roles Work Together

```
Davis creates the ads and content that drive demand
    ↓
Alex builds the funnels and tools that capture and qualify that demand
    ↓
Sasha buys the media that distributes the creative
    and closes the attorney partnerships that monetize the leads
    ↓
Revenue flows back → 33/33/33
```
