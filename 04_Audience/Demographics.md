# Demographics: Second Chair

## Target Audience Overview

**Motor Vehicle Accident (MVA) victims** who may have legal claims and need representation.

Second Chair focuses exclusively on MVA â€” we do not target workplace injuries, slip and fall, medical malpractice, or other PI categories.
