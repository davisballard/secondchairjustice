# Google Ad Formats: Second Chair

## Search Ads

### Responsive Search Ads
| Element | Limit | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| **Headlines** | Up to 15 | 30 characters each |
| **Descriptions** | Up to 4 | 90 characters each |
| **Display Path** | 2 fields | 15 characters each |
| **Final URL** | 1 | Landing page URL |

### Recommended Headlines
Use approved phrases from `Approved_Phrases.md`:
- "Car Accident Cases Being Accepted"
- "Speak With an Attorney Today"
- "Free Case Evaluation"
- "No Upfront Cost"
- "Legal Help Available Now"

### Recommended Descriptions
- "Accident victims may be entitled to compensation. Connect with experienced attorneys at no upfront cost."
- "Settlements in similar cases have reached significant amounts. See if you qualify for a free evaluation."

## Second Chair Specific Requirements

### Identity
- Clearly identify as Second Chair (lead generation)
- Do NOT claim to be a law firm
- Do NOT misrepresent

### Visual Compliance
- No injury/medical imagery
- May trigger healthcare filter
- Stick to abstract legal symbols
- See `Banned_Imagery.md`

### Copy Compliance
- Third-person language only
- Qualified claims only
- See `Third_Person_Rules.md`

---

## Targeting Notes

### Legal Services Restrictions
- Cannot target based on health conditions
- Cannot use personalized advertising for health
- Broad targeting recommended
- Let creative qualify audience

### Recommended Targeting
- Geographic (state-based)
- Age 18+
- Language
- Contextual (legal topics)
