# LinkedIn Ad Formats: Second Chair

> **Platform Focus:** Professional network, B2B primary but personal injury can work for workplace-adjacent accidents.
> 
> **Key Insight:** LinkedIn users expect professional, informative content. Educational angles work best.

---

## Ad Copy Fields

| Field | Character Limit | Notes |
|-------|-----------------|-------|
| **Introductory Text** | 600 characters | Primary copy (150 char before truncation) |
| **Headline** | 70 characters | Below image |
| **Description** | 100 characters | Below headline |
| **CTA Button** | Standard options | "Learn More" for lead gen |

### CTA Button Options
- Learn More
- Sign Up
- Register
- Download
- Apply
- Get Quote
- Contact Us

---

## Sponsored Content (Feed)

### Single Image
| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Dimensions** | 1200 x 627px (1.91:1) recommended |
| **Alternative** | 1080 x 1080px (1:1) |
| **File Size** | Max 8MB |
| **Format** | JPG or PNG |

### Carousel
| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Dimensions** | 1080 x 1080px (1:1) per card |
| **Cards** | 2-10 images |
| **File Size** | Max 10MB per card |

### Video
| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Dimensions** | 1920 x 1080px (16:9) or 1080 x 1080px (1:1) |
| **Duration** | 3 seconds - 30 minutes (15-30 sec optimal) |
| **File Size** | Max 200MB |
| **Format** | MP4 |

## Creative Best Practices

### What Works
- Educational, informative content
- Statistics and data points
- Professional but accessible tone
- Clear value proposition
- Thought leadership angle

### What Doesn't Work
- Aggressive sales messaging
- Emotional manipulation
- Unprofessional visuals
- Clickbait headlines

---

## Second Chair Specific Requirements

### Audience Consideration
LinkedIn users are professionals. Angle toward:
- Workplace-adjacent accidents (commuting)
- Professional reputation concerns
- Time/productivity loss
- Financial impact on career

### Compliance
- Professional tone required
- Clear disclaimers
- No sensationalism

### Targeting
- Geographic: State-based
- Industry: Can target specific industries
- Job Function: All relevant
- Age: 25-55 optimal

---

## Recommended Messaging Angles

| Angle | Why It Works on LinkedIn |
|-------|-------------------------|
| **Financial Impact** | Professionals understand ROI |
| **Time Recovery** | Busy professionals value time |
| **Rights Education** | Information-seeking audience |
| **Process Clarity** | Appreciate structured information |
