# TikTok Ad Formats: Second Chair

> **Platform Focus:** Native, lo-fi content that blends with organic feed.
> 
> **Key Insight:** TikTok rewards authenticity. Polished ads underperform.

---

## Ad Copy Fields

| Field | Character Limit | Notes |
|-------|-----------------|-------|
| **Ad Text** | 100 characters | Primary caption, visible immediately |
| **CTA Button** | Standard options | "Learn More" recommended for lead gen |
| **Display Name** | 20 characters | "Claim Justice Now" or similar |

### CTA Button Options
- Learn More
- Sign Up
- Contact Us
- Apply Now
- Get Quote

---

## Video Specs

### In-Feed Ads
| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Dimensions** | 1080 x 1920px (9:16) |
| **Aspect Ratio** | 9:16 (vertical only) |
| **Duration** | 5-60 seconds (15-30 sec optimal) |
| **File Size** | Max 500MB |
| **Format** | MP4 or MOV |
| **Audio** | Required (sound-on platform) |

### Spark Ads (Boosted Organic)
| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Source** | Existing organic TikTok post |
| **Duration** | Any length |
| **Authenticity** | Highest performer |

---

## Image Specs (Carousel)

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Dimensions** | 1080 x 1920px (9:16) |
| **Cards** | 2-10 images |
| **Format** | JPG or PNG |

## Creative Best Practices

### What Works
- First-person POV
- "Storytime" format
- Green screen over documents
- Car rant format
- Native text overlays (TikTok fonts)
- Trending sounds (when appropriate)

### What Doesn't Work
- Polished production
- Corporate aesthetics
- Horizontal video
- Stock footage
- Burnt-in professional subtitles

---

## Second Chair Specific Requirements

### Compliance
- "Attorney Advertising" disclosure in caption
- No specific dollar guarantees
- Third-person language for claims

### Visual Guidelines
- Lo-fi, authentic aesthetic
- POV perspectives preferred
- No blood/injury imagery
- Recovery/hope themes

### Targeting
- Age: 18+
- Geographic: State-based
- Interest: Broad (legal topics, news)
