## Key Principle

**Everything here is market-agnostic.** State-specific pain points, regulatory nuances, and competitive dynamics go in the state folders (e.g., `01_New_York/`).

This folder contains the **universal truths** that apply to every PI attorney in every market:
- They want cases, not clicks
- They care about cost-per-signed-case, not CPL
- They have pride in their work and hate being called ambulance chasers
- They're fighting well-funded insurance companies
- They need volume but also need quality

Build state-specific creative ON TOP OF this foundation.
