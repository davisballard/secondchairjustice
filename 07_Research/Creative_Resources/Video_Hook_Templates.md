# Video Hook Templates: Second Chair

> **200 Proven Video Hook Formulas for TikTok, Reels, and YouTube Shorts**
> 
> Fill-in-the-blank templates optimized for scroll-stopping video content. Adapt to PI/legal context.

---

## Usage Notes

- **First 3 seconds are everything** — the hook IS the ad
- **Match hooks to injury type and audience** — see `Hooks_And_Angles.md` for angle pairing
- **Lo-fi delivery** — read these naturally, not scripted/polished
- **Compliance check** — run controversial hooks by @Saul before production

## Production Notes

- **Delivery:** Conversational, not scripted — these should feel spontaneous
- **Setting:** Car talks, walk-and-talks, office desk — lo-fi environments
- **Length:** Hook in first 3 seconds, total video **6-15 seconds** (see below)
- **CTA:** Soft ask ("Link in bio for free case review") not hard sell

### Video Format Strategy

| Format | Duration | Clips | When to Use |
|--------|----------|-------|-------------|
| **Motion Still** | 5-6s | 1 | Single scroll-stopping image with subtle motion |
| **TikTok Slide** | 15s | 3 | Story sequence: Hook → Action → CTA |
