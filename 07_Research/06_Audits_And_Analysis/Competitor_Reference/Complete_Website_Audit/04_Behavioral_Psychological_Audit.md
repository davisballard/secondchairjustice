## BJ Fogg Behavior Model: B = MAT

**Behavior happens when Motivation, Ability, and Trigger converge.**

**Current State Analysis:**

**MOTIVATION:** Medium-High
- Pain acknowledged: "Your competitors are bidding"
- Benefit clear: "Predictable case flow"
- Differentiation strong: "Geo-exclusive"
- **Gap:** No amplification of urgency or emotion

**ABILITY:** Medium
- Easy: Single application form
- Hard: Unknown commitment level
- Hard: Switching from current vendor
- Hard: Unclear approval process or timeline

**TRIGGERS:** Weak
- CTAs present but not at peak motivation
- No countdown or deadline triggers
- No "someone from your city applied" triggers
- No reminder or retargeting mechanisms visible

**VERDICT:** Motivation and ability are present, but triggers are not optimally timed or compelling enough to push visitors over the action line.
