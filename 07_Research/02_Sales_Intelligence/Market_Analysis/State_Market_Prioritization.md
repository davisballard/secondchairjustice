2. **High willingness to pay** (premium pricing)
3. **Sophisticated buyers** (understand ROI, buy faster, stay longer)
4. **Acute pain** (desperate = urgent = shorter sales cycle)
5. **Complex environment** (regulatory barriers favor specialists)

**The Top 5 states (CA, FL, TX, NY, IL) dominate on ALL five factors.** This is why they should receive 80% of focus.

### The Bottom Line

**Go where the money is.** That means:
- Large markets with high firm density
- Expensive advertising markets (high willingness to pay)
- Sophisticated buyers who track cost-per-case
- Firms in pain (Google Ads bleeding them dry)
