## NYC Market Notes

### Market Characteristics
- **Serious injury threshold complexity:** NY is no-fault state, plaintiffs can only sue if injury meets "serious injury" threshold (fracture, permanent loss of use, significant limitation, etc.)
- **Highest Google CPC in nation:** $400-500/click for PI keywords
- **Sophisticated insurance defense:** NYC firms face the most well-funded, aggressive insurance defense attorneys in the country
- **High overhead, high stakes:** NYC operating costs are 40-50% higher than national average, making cost-per-signed-case critical

### Sales Lead
**Primary:** Lead salesperson based in NYC with deep market knowledge and existing relationships in NY legal community

### Best Messaging Angles
1. **Serious Injury Threshold Expertise:** "We understand NY's serious injury threshold. Every lead is pre-qualified for fractures, surgeries, or ongoing significant limitations. No wasted intake time on cases that won't clear the bar."

2. **Google CPC Arbitrage:** "Google Ads in NYC: $400-$500/click. Our exclusive, qualified leads: $400. Which one converts better?"

3. **Geo-Exclusive Territory:** "One firm per market. No bidding wars. You get exclusive territory rightsâ€”if they're in your area, they're only calling you."

4. **Cost Per Signed Case ROI:** "Cost per signed case via Second Chair: $1,600-$2,400. Via Google Ads in NYC: $8,000-$20,000+. The arbitrage is enormous."

### Key Differentiators for NYC
- Deep understanding of NY's no-fault system and serious injury threshold
- Pre-qualification prevents wasted intake time on non-viable cases
- Exclusive territory = no shared lead race-to-phone dynamics
