## PLATFORM: FAL (LOCAL API)

**What:** Direct API access to video/image models
**Why:** Cost control, bulk generation, iteration speed

### Available Video Models

| Model | Best For | Duration |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **Kling 2.6** | High quality, physics, general purpose | 5-10s |
| **Kling Turbo** | Fast iteration, testing | 5s |
| **Hailuo (Minimax)** | Motion quality, consistent faces | 5-10s |
| **Wan 2.6** | Alternative aesthetics | 5-10s |

### Available Image Models

| Model | Best For |
|-------|----------|
| **FLUX 1.1 Pro** | Fast, general purpose |
| **FLUX Dev** | Development/testing |
| **Kling Image** | Photorealistic |

### When to Use FAL
- Bulk generation (10+ variations)
- Tight cost tracking needed
- Testing concepts before final
- Building multi-image sequences
- Any standard image/video generation

### Workflow
1. Concept → Generate multiple variations via FAL
2. Review → Pick best direction
3. Polish → Either refine in FAL or use Higgsfield special features

## PLATFORM: HIGGSFIELD

**What:** Web platform with special features not available elsewhere
**URL:** higgsfield.ai

### Unique Features (Use Higgsfield For These)

| Feature | What It Does | Use When |
|---------|--------------|----------|
| **Angles 2.0** | Generate multiple camera angles from one image | Need front/side/back shots of same subject |
| **Motion Control** | Copy motion from reference video to AI character | Want specific gestures/movements from a reference |
| **Cinema Studio** | Simulate real camera sensors + lenses | Need cinematic look (ARRI, Cooke, specific focal lengths) |
| **Click to Ad** | URL → auto-generated video ad | Quick product video from landing page |
| **Bullet Time** | 360° product spin | Product showcase |
| **Soul ID** | Consistent AI character across generations | Need same person in multiple videos |

### Video Models Available

| Model | Best For |
|-------|----------|
| **Kling 2.6** | High quality, general purpose |
| **Sora 2** | OpenAI's model, cinematic |
| **Veo 3.1** | Google's model, natural motion |
| **Wan 2.6** | Alternative aesthetics |
| **Hailuo 02** | Motion quality |

### Image Models Available

| Model | Best For |
|-------|----------|
| **Nano Banana Pro** | 4K, fast, general purpose (unlimited on subscription) |
| **Soul** | Fashion/lifestyle, consistent characters |
| **FLUX.2** | Speed, physics accuracy |
| **GPT Image 1.5** | Artistic interpretation |

### When to Use Higgsfield
- Need multi-angle shots (Angles 2.0)
- Need to match motion from a reference (Motion Control)
- Need cinematic camera/lens simulation (Cinema Studio)
- Testing with unlimited Nano Banana
- Quick product ad from URL (Click to Ad)

### When FAL is Better
- Bulk generation (cost tracking)
- Simple image/video (no special features needed)
- Need specific API control

## PLATFORM: ARGIL

**URL:** argil.ai
**Account:** Free account available, login with Google

### What Argil Does Best

| Use Case | Why Argil |
|----------|-----------|
| **Clean regular UGC AI** | Looks professional, not obviously AI |
| **Complex b-roll videos (35s+)** | ~10 credits, allows b-roll inclusion |
| **Character swap** | Record yourself → swap in another actor |
| **Full ads with VO** | Combines talking head + b-roll + audio |

### Cost
- ~10 credits per complex video (cheap)
- Much better value than Arcads ($300/month for 5-10 videos)

### Workflow
1. Write script / talking points
2. Select or upload actor
3. Add b-roll if needed
4. Generate
5. Download and deploy

### Character Swap Feature
- Record yourself doing the action/movement
- Upload to Argil
- Swap in a different actor/face
- Motion and performance transfers to new character

### Best For
- Guidance-focused ads
- Settlement explanation videos
- Professional spokesperson content
- Longer-form content (35s+)

## PLATFORM: YAPPER

**Purpose:** Outlandish, creative, "retardmax" talking head content

### What Yapper Does Best

| Use Case | Why Yapper |
|----------|------------|
| **Outlandish/creative content** | Weird shit that stands out |
| **"Retardmax" style** | Shows range, memorable |
| **Mixing it up** | Different look from Argil |
| **Attention-grabbing hooks** | Unusual = scroll-stopping |

### Philosophy (Danny)
> "People wanna do business with those who can retardmax like us"

Mixing clean UGC (Argil) with outlandish content (Yapper) shows range and makes content more memorable.

### Best For
- Creative hooks
- Pattern interrupt content
- When you want to stand out
- Variety in content mix

## EXECUTION FORMAT DETAILS

### 1. Still Image
**Tools:** FAL (FLUX, Kling), Midjourney, Higgsfield (Nano Banana)
**Use When:**
- Static ad placements
- Thumbnails
- Hero images for landing pages
- Social posts

---

### 2. Animated Image (5-10s)
**Tools:** FAL (Kling, Hailuo), Higgsfield
**Use When:**
- Want motion from a still
- Subtle movement (breathing, slight camera move)
- Ken Burns style
- Video placements but simple content

---

### 3. Cinematic Video
**Tools:** Higgsfield (Cinema Studio), FAL (Kling 2.6)
**Use When:**
- Need high-end commercial look
- Specific camera/lens simulation
- Dramatic lighting/depth of field
- Premium brand feel

**Higgsfield Cinema Studio Options:**
- Camera sensors: ARRI Alexa, RED, Sony Venice
- Lens glass: Cooke, Zeiss, Panavision
- Focal lengths: 24mm, 35mm, 50mm, 85mm, etc.

---

### 4. Multi-Angle Shot
**Tools:** Higgsfield (Angles 2.0) — ONLY HERE
**Use When:**
- Need front/side/back of same subject
- Product from multiple angles
- Character turnaround
- One image → multiple perspectives

---

### 5. Motion-Matched Video
**Tools:** Higgsfield (Motion Control) — ONLY HERE
**Use When:**
- Have reference video with motion you want to copy
- Want specific gestures/movements
- "Puppeteering" AI character with real motion
- Matching performance across takes

---

### 6. UGC Talking Head — Clean
**Tools:** Argil
**Use When:**
- Professional appearance needed
- Guidance/educational content
- Trust-building content
- Settlement explanations

---

### 7. UGC Talking Head — Outlandish
**Tools:** Yapper
**Use When:**
- Creative/attention-grabbing needed
- Pattern interrupt
- Memorable/shareable content
- "Retardmax" energy

---

### 8. Full Video w/ VO + B-roll
**Tools:** Argil (primary), or FAL clips + manual edit
**Use When:**
- Complete ad needed
- Voiceover with visuals
- B-roll integration
- Spokesperson + supporting footage

---

### 9. Character Swap
**Tools:** Argil
**Use When:**
- You want your performance but different face
- Consistent motion across multiple actors
- Testing different demographics
- Scaling content with one recording

---

### 10. Multi-Image Sequence
**Tools:** FAL + Video Editor, Higgsfield + Video Editor
**Use When:**
- Story told through progression
- Before/after style
- Step-by-step visualization
- Multiple scenes needed
