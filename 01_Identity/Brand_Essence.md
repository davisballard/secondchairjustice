# Brand Essence: Second Chair

## What We Are

Second Chair is a **geo-exclusive lead generation company** specializing in **personal injury (PI)** for law firms. We are NOT a law firm — we are a demand infrastructure provider with an advertising-first approach.

### Practice Areas

**Personal Injury (PI/MVA)** — The full case spectrum:

- **"Keep the Lights On" cases** — Soft tissue (whiplash, neck/back), minor fractures, general MVA with documented injuries. Consistent, quality volume that keeps a practice alive.
- **"Build the Firm" cases** — Commercial truck/18-wheeler ($100K–$1M+), rideshare/Uber/Lyft (stacked insurance layers), serious spinal/back injuries ($200K–$3.6M+), pedestrian/cyclist incidents, wrongful death.

We do NOT generate leads for:
- Workplace injuries
- Slip and fall
- Medical malpractice
- Immigration (removed from scope)
- Mass tort (removed from scope)

### The Business Model

**Stream 1 — Standard Leads ($400/lead, Day 1):**
1. Second Chair runs its own ad accounts on Meta + Google
2. Ads are *generic* (not attorney-specific) — third-person language only
3. Leads are captured via behaviorally-optimized landing pages and quiz funnels
4. Leads are sold/sent exclusively to one partner law firm per DMA
5. Consumer brand: **Claim Justice Now** (claimjusticenow.com)

**Stream 2 — Premium Case-Ready Leads ($1,500+/lead, Month 4–6):**
1. Leads go through a full nurturing stack before reaching the attorney
2. AI call bots, AI chatbots, SMS/text sequences, real human calls for high-value cases
3. Full intake documentation — accident details, injury info, insurance status, treatment records
4. Lead arrives at the attorney's desk case-ready — they review and sign
5. 4–5x revenue per lead vs. standard

**Stream 3 — Agency Services for Attorney Partners (Month 4–6):**
1. Brand identity development, ad campaign creation and management
2. AI chatbot builds, email/nurture flows, website builds
3. Ad buying and media management for partner firms
4. Upsell to existing partners who already trust our work — deepens relationships and creates switching costs
